On-device editing

Your photos stay on your phone.

Object erasing and background removal are processed on your device, so you can edit personal photos without handing them to a server.

  • Erase objects and cut out subjects right on your phone.
  • Your drawings and backups are stored on your device.
  • Export a full backup any time and restore it on a new phone.

For details on how Drawwii handles your data, see our Privacy Policy.
