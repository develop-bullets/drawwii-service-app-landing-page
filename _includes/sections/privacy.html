Privacy by design

Your photos stay on your phone.

Object erasing and background removal run entirely on-device with LaMa inpainting and on-device subject segmentation.

  • No account, no sign-in, no cloud sync.
  • Your photos, drawings, and backups never touch our servers.
  • Free to download — no subscriptions, no in-app purchases.

Magic Eraser downloads its ~198 MB AI model once, over the internet. After that, it runs on your device.

Drawwii collects anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. It never includes your images, and you can turn it off in Settings. See our Privacy Policy.
