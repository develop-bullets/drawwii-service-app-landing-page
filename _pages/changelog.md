## 26.27 — Templates, tools, and stickers

**A whole design library, and a toolbox that's easier to find your way around.**

- **290+ design templates across 20+ categories** — planners, checklists, invitations, celebrations, business, travel, food, pets, and more.
- **40+ social size presets** — Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, X, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and others, plus new cover and banner ratios.
- **Vector stickers** — a built-in SVG sticker set, with a little confetti when you save.
- **Cutouts you can keep drawing with** — the subject you cut out of a photo becomes a movable, resizable element in the drawing editor, and you can tone-adjust it on its own.
- **Batch background removal** — remove the background from several photos at once.
- **Tools tab, redesigned** — search across every tool, and a denser two-column grid.
- **Japanese support** — Drawwii is now available in English, Korean, and Japanese, and you can switch language manually in Settings.
- **A new onboarding** — including a before/after demo of the Magic Eraser.
- More collage layouts, a mat frame style, and a tiled watermark mode.
- Fixes: batch background removal no longer hangs, collage no longer crashes on entry, and document scanning asks for camera permission correctly.

## 26.24 — Collage and a smarter eraser

- **Collage** — combine photos in grid or free layouts, then draw on top.
- **Smarter Magic Eraser** — auto-select objects, mark with a brush or wand, and compare before/after.
- **Daily prompts & streaks** — get a fresh idea each day and keep your drawing streak alive.

## 26.23 — Brushes and export

- **Brush opacity** — adjust how transparent your brush is.
- **Recent colors** — quickly reuse colors you just picked.
- **Export options** — choose transparent PNG, JPG, and resolution.
